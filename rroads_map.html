Data and Methods

The data came from Neil Holbrook and Richard Morton, who compiled archaeogical work on sites with Roman remains between 1990 and 2004. An England boundary was divided into regular squares, and spatially joined to the archaeological data. Main Roman towns were extracted from Margary's Roman Towns, courtesy of Mike Bishop, resulting in a map showcasing the link between the number of archaeological Roman sites and main Roman towns.
